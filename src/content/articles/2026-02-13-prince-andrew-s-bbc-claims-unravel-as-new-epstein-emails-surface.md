Newly released Epstein emails appear to contradict statements Prince Andrew made during his 2019 BBC Newsnight interview, according to a BBC analysis published this week.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> The BBC published a video comparison of Andrew's interview statements and the newly released documents.

Andrew settled a civil lawsuit with Virginia Giuffre in February 2022 for a reported 12 million pounds without admitting wrongdoing.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> He has not commented publicly since the settlement. Buckingham Palace has not addressed the newly released documents.

## What Andrew Stated in the Interview

In the November 2019 Newsnight interview with Emily Maitlis, Andrew denied Giuffre's allegations. He stated he could not have been at a London nightclub with her because he was at a Pizza Express in Woking that evening.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> He also stated he did not sweat due to a medical condition related to the Falklands War — a response to Giuffre's account that he was perspiring while dancing with her.

Andrew stepped back from royal duties within days of the interview.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>
