**Don Huffines**, a Republican candidate for Texas comptroller who describes himself as a "MAGA Trump Republican," owns **Jeffrey Epstein's former Zorro Ranch** in New Mexico, according to property records. The revelation comes as Rep. **Thomas Massie** continues pressing the Department of Justice over what he characterizes as incomplete disclosure of Epstein-related documents, despite a federal law mandating their release.

Huffines acquired the **10,000-acre property** in Stanley, New Mexico, where Epstein allegedly abused underage girls, according to The Independent. The ranch served as one of Epstein's primary residences before his 2019 death in federal custody. Texas GOP officials [expressed alarm over the purchase](/news/2026-02-15-texas-gop-alarmed-over-comptroller-candidate-s-epstein-ranch-purchase) earlier this month.

The property purchase has created complications for Huffines's campaign in a race where Republican primary voters have shown intense interest in Epstein-related issues. Huffines has not publicly addressed when he purchased the property or his intentions for the site.
