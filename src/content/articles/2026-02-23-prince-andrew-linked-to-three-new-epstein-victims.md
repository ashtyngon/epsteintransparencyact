Three additional women have accused **Prince Andrew** of sexual abuse connected to **Jeffrey Epstein**, according to attorneys representing the accusers, Reality Tea reported.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

The lawyers disclosed that three more women have come forward with claims involving the Duke of York.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> The attorneys did not publicly identify the women or provide specific details about the alleged encounters.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> No formal legal action has been filed at this time.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

Andrew has denied all allegations of sexual misconduct related to Epstein. In 2022, he settled a civil lawsuit brought by **Virginia Giuffre**, who alleged that Epstein and **Ghislaine Maxwell** trafficked her to Andrew when she was 17.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> The settlement included no admission of liability.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

## Royal Ties to Convicted Sex Offender

Andrew's connections to Epstein date back decades. The prince was photographed with Epstein on multiple occasions, including after Epstein's 2008 conviction on state charges of soliciting a minor in Florida.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> Andrew maintained the friendship even after Epstein became a registered sex offender.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

The **Metropolitan Police** [recently appealed](/news/2026-02-23-police-appeal-to-prince-andrew-s-former-guards-over-epstein-inquiry) to former royal protection officers who guarded Andrew to come forward with information relevant to their investigation into individuals connected to Epstein.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> In February 2026, [British authorities arrested Andrew](/news/2026-02-19-prince-andrew-arrested-in-london-over-epstein-documents) in connection with the Epstein investigation.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

Andrew stepped back from royal duties in 2019 following a BBC interview in which he addressed his friendship with Epstein.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> He was stripped of his military titles and royal patronages by **Queen Elizabeth II** in early 2022, shortly before the Giuffre lawsuit was settled.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

## Maxwell Conviction

Maxwell, Epstein's longtime associate, was convicted in December 2021 of sex trafficking minors for Epstein and is serving a 20-year federal prison sentence.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> During her trial, multiple victims testified about being abused by Epstein and trafficked to his associates.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> Andrew was not charged with any crimes.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

Epstein died by suicide in a Manhattan jail cell in August 2019 while awaiting trial on federal sex trafficking charges.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> He had been arrested on charges that he sexually exploited and abused dozens of minor girls at his residences in New York and Florida.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

Andrew has not publicly responded to the new allegations.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>
