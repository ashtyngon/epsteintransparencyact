**Peter Attia**, a physician known for his work on longevity and metabolic health, has parted ways with **CBS News** following the disclosure of his name in documents related to Jeffrey Epstein, according to Deadline.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

The media outlet reported that Attia's departure from his contributor role came after his connection to Epstein emerged in recently released files.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> CBS News confirmed the end of the working relationship.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

Attia had served as a medical contributor for CBS News, providing analysis and commentary on health-related topics.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> His media presence extended beyond television, as he hosts a popular podcast focused on medicine and human performance.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>
