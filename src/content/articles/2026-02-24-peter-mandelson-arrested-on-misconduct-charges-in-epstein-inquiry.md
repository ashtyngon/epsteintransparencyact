Peter **Mandelson**, the former UK ambassador to the United States, was arrested Monday on suspicion of misconduct in public office over allegations he sent confidential government information to convicted sex offender **Jeffrey Epstein**, according to BBC News special correspondent **Lucy Manning**.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

The arrest follows the release of files related to Epstein, though the specific documents that prompted the investigation have not been publicly detailed.<sup>[2](#ref-2)</sup> Mandelson served as UK ambassador to the U.S. until recently, a position he assumed in 2025 before his [arrest in February 2026](/news/2026-02-23-peter-mandelson-from-us-ambassador-to-arrest-in-six-months).<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

## Charges and Allegations

The misconduct in public office charge centers on allegations that Mandelson shared confidential government information with Epstein while serving in public positions.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup> Manning reported the arrest and the nature of the allegations during CBS News coverage.<sup>[1](#ref-1)</sup>

British authorities have not disclosed the timeframe during which the alleged sharing of information occurred, nor have they specified which government positions Mandelson held at the time of the alleged conduct. Mandelson previously served in multiple UK government roles before his appointment as ambassador.

## Background on Epstein Connections

Epstein was convicted in 2008 on state charges of soliciting a minor in Florida. He died in federal custody in August 2019 while awaiting trial on federal sex trafficking charges.

The arrest follows [earlier reporting on Mandelson facing misconduct charges](/news/2026-02-23-peter-mandelson-faces-misconduct-charge-in-epstein-investigation) related to the Epstein investigation. The case represents one of multiple ongoing inquiries into individuals connected to Epstein following the release of court documents and records related to his criminal cases.

British police have not announced additional arrests or charges in connection with the investigation into Mandelson's alleged conduct.
