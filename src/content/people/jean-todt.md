According to his Wikipedia biography, he served as general manager of Scuderia Ferrari's racing division from 1993 to 2007 and was appointed Ferrari's chief executive in 2004, before becoming the ninth president of the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA), motorsport's world governing body, from October 2009 until December 2021.
