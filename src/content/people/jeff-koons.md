## Is Jeff Koons in the Epstein files?

Jeff Koons (Contemporary artist; known for large-scale sculpture and painting; "Rabbit" (1986) sold at Christie's for a world-record $91.1 million in 2019) is named in connection with the Jeffrey Epstein files.
