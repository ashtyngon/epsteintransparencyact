## Is Michael Ovitz in the Epstein files?

Yes. Michael Ovitz (Co-founder of Creative Artists Agency (CAA); former President of The Walt Disney Company) is named in connection with the Jeffrey Epstein files. DOJ-released documents show email correspondence with Epstein spanning 2010–2013, years after Epstein's 2008 guilty plea. In 2010 Ovitz emailed Epstein following a dinner they had both attended with former Barclays CEO Jes Staley. In April 2012 he wrote to Epstein that he would drop by the financier's Manhattan townhouse for a drink. In December 2012 Epstein invited Ovitz to his private Caribbean island, which Ovitz declined. Epstein also told others — including his publicist Peggy Siegal — that he knew Ovitz "well." A spokesperson for Ovitz stated: "They had very limited interaction and correspondence.
