Vitaly Ivanovich Churkin was a Russian career diplomat. According to his Wikipedia biography, he was born in Moscow on February 21, 1952, and served as Russia's Permanent Representative to the United Nations from May 1, 2006, until his death on February 20, 2017. Before the UN posting, he held several ambassadorial roles, including Ambassador to Belgium and Ambassador to Canada, and served as a Deputy Foreign Minister. He died in New York City at the age of 64.
