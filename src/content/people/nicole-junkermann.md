Nicole Junkermann is a German businesswoman and investor. According to her Wikipedia article, she founded NJF Holdings, an international investment firm with interests in venture capital, private equity, and real estate, and she has held positions including trustee of the Royal Marsden Cancer Charity and, from September 2025, visiting professor at Lancaster University. The Wikipedia entry that lists people named in the files describes her as "a German entrepreneur and investor."
