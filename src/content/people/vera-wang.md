## Is Vera Wang in the Epstein files?

Yes. Vera Wang (Fashion designer; founder of Vera Wang bridal and fashion brand) is named in connection with the Jeffrey Epstein files. Contributed a personal letter to Jeffrey Epstein's birthday book "The First Fifty Years," a 238-page scrapbook assembled by Ghislaine Maxwell to mark Epstein's 50th birthday in 2003; the book was released by the House Oversight Committee in 2025 as part of the Epstein estate documents. Wang's letter is reported to have suggested a shopping trip and joked about Epstein appearing on The Bachelor television series; her contribution was filed among sections of the book reflecting Epstein's social and personal circle. Additionally, documents and reporting published between 2025 and 2026 document that Epstein name-dropped Wang's name — along with supermodel Naomi Campbell's — to recruits and contacts in the fashion industry, presenting himself as well-connected to leading figures in the field in order to gain trust. Wang told the Wall Street Journal she regretted her association with Epstein and had no knowledge of his crimes. No wrongdoing is alleged against her.
