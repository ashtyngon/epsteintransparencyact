Joshua Cooper Ramo is an author and foreign-affairs analyst. According to his Wikipedia article, he is vice chairman and co-chief executive of Kissinger Associates, the consulting firm founded by former U.S. Secretary of State Henry Kissinger. He has written several books, including *The Age of the Unthinkable* (2009) and *The Seventh Sense* (2016), two New York Times bestsellers, and he serves on the boards of Starbucks and FedEx.

Ramo's documented appearance in connection with [Jeffrey Epstein](/people/jeffrey-epstein) stems from Epstein's calendar. As reported by Global News, the Wall Street Journal obtained Epstein's calendar showing that he scheduled meetings with a range of prominent figures. According to the Wikipedia article on Ramo, the Wall Street Journal reported in April 2023 that Ramo held more than a dozen meetings with Epstein from 2013 to 2017, and that many of those meetings took place at Epstein's New York townhouse after 5 p.m. The same article states that a September 2013 breakfast at the townhouse included former Israeli Prime Minister Ehud Barak, and that on another occasion the two met with Ariane de Rothschild, then chief executive of the Swiss private bank Edmond de Rothschild Group.

According to the Wikipedia article on Ramo, he "did not provide comment for the story." No public response from Ramo or Kissinger Associates is reflected in the sources reviewed for this profile.
