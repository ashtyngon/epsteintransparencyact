According to his Wikipedia biography, Gordon Peter Getty (born December 20, 1933) is "an American businessman and classical music composer, the fourth child of oil tycoon J. Paul Getty." He assumed control of the family's multibillion-dollar trust in 1976 and oversaw the 1986 sale of Getty Oil to Texaco. Alongside his business career he is an accomplished classical composer of opera, choral, and chamber works, including *Plump Jack*, *Usher House*, and *The Canterville Ghost*.

Getty's own Wikipedia article, citing the Epstein files released in 2026, reports that the email exchanges began in 2013, five years after Epstein's 2008 Florida conviction. According to the same article, the two remained on email threads until April 2019, though it is unclear whether personal communication continued.
