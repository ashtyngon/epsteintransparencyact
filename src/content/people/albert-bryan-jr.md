Albert Bryan Jr. (born February 21, 1968) is a U.S. Virgin Islander politician and former businessman who has served since January 7, 2019, as the ninth Governor of the U.S. Virgin Islands. A Democrat, he previously served as Commissioner of the territory's Department of Labor and was reelected governor in 2022.
