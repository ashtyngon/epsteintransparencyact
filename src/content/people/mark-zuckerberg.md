Mark Zuckerberg is an American businessman and programmer who co-founded Facebook — now Meta Platforms — in 2004 and serves as its chairman and chief executive officer, according to his Wikipedia biography. He is also known for the Chan Zuckerberg Initiative, a philanthropic effort he runs with his wife, Priscilla Chan.

Zuckerberg does not have his own standalone entry on Wikipedia's "List of people named in the Epstein files." His name appears only within the entries of other people — specifically those of MIT neuroscientist Edward Boyden and LinkedIn co-founder Reid Hoffman.
